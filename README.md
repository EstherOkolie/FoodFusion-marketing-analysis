### 1. Campaign Reach & Conversion Analysis

* Tracked **total audience reached**, **total conversions**, and **retained customers** across channels
* Monitored **monthly and weekly trends** to identify performance fluctuations
* Calculated **Month-over-Month (MoM)** changes in conversions and retention

### 2. Customer Segmentation Analysis

### 3. Retention Analysis

* Compared retained vs non-retained customers across channels
